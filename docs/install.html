tinyblok installer

Flash tinyblok from a Chromium-based browser, then configure Wi-Fi and NATS from the device setup portal.

  1. Connect the ESP32-C6 over USB.
  2. Click install and choose the serial port.
  3. After flashing, join the TINYBLOK Wi-Fi network.
  4. Open the captive portal, or browse to http://10.42.0.1.
  5. Save Wi-Fi, device name, and NATS settings. No per-user secrets are baked into firmware.
